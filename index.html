What is inside the sea?

Coverage and Volume

  • The ocean covers over 70% of the Earth's surface.
  • It holds about 97% of Earth's water.

Climate Regulation

  • The ocean absorbs and distributes solar heat, playing a key role in regulating global climate.
  • It acts as a major carbon sink, absorbing significant amounts of carbon dioxide from the atmosphere.

Biodiversity

  • The ocean is home to an estimated 230,000 known species, with potentially millions more yet to be discovered.
  • Marine ecosystems include coral reefs, deep-sea trenches, and open ocean habitats
